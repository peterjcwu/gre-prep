## Question

The agin engineer found it strange that the (i) ______ etching process she studied as a student of technology was still considered (ii) ______ today.

## Options

### Block (i)

    [A] porous
    [B] innovative
    [C] implausible

### Block (ii)

    [D] antiquated
    [E] translucent
    [F] novel

## Answer

    [B] innovative
    [F] novel
