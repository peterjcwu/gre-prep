# loquacious

/ləˈkweɪ.ʃəs/

*top 50 gre words*

(adjective) Someone who is loquacious talks a lot

## Example

Edwina, Williams’ mother, was judgmental, frigid and pious, but also as loquacious as her husband was laconic.

## Synonyms

+ chatty
+ garrulous
+ disapproving
+ talkative
+ verbose 
+ voluble 
+ wordy
